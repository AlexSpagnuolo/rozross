SPICE Resiliency Program
Spiritual, Physical, Intellectual, Creative, Emotional.

We hear a lot about the importance of resiliency and work life balance, but what if there could be more? What if you could easily and systematically create balance and resiliency? Think of it as spicing up your life!

The spice program is designed to build your capacity to adapt to change and rediscover the things that make you uniquely you.
