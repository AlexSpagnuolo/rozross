Executive Coaching
As an executive myself, I have a keen sense of the opportunities and challenges experienced by senior leaders in the world today.

The benefits of coaching include

  • Increased productivity
  • Reduced stress
  • Strengthened leadership skills
  • Improved relationships
  • Tangible results

By providing a confidential space to talk about your goals and plans, together we will work to clarify these and to determine the steps you will take to achieve them. Leveraging a coaching methodology we will structure our conversations so that you will achieve the maximum benefits for your unique needs. Together we will personalize a program for you based on our initial conversation.

Programs may include

  • Regular coaching conversations
    (in person, by phone or by video-conferencing)
  • Pre and Post 360 degree feedback exercise to measure results
  • Emotional Intelligence reporting
  • Application of various analytical tools
  • Focus on specific skills development
