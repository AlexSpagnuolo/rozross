100 Day Journey
A lot can be accomplished in 100 days.

A Coaching engagement with a duration of 100 days will allow us to focus for a defined length of time on something which is important to you. This program is ideal for someone who is starting a new job or project. It is also valuable for someone who is looking to achieve a goal or realize a change.

I provide a confidential space to talk about your goals and plans, together we will work to clarify these and to determine the steps you will take to achieve them.

The 100 day journey consists of 10 one hour sessions at a reduced rate.

Programs may include

  • Regular coaching conversations
    (in person, by phone or by video-conferencing)
  • Pre and Post 360 degree feedback exercise to measure results
  • Emotional Intelligence reporting
  • Application of various analytical tools
  • Focus on specific skills development
